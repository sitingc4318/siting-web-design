Recipe of Coca Cola Chicken Wings

Ingredient:

* One bottle of Coca Cola

* Some Chicken Wing

* Ginger, Scallion, Garlic

* Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce, Cooking Wing

Steps:

1)Peel the outside skin of ginger and cut into ginger slice.

2)Peel the garlic and chopped it into small cube,

3)Get a long Scallion and tied the whole piece up into a knot.

4)Get the chicken wing out and washed it cleanly.

5)Make three cut on the front and back of the wing's skin so all the flavor will go into it.

6)Put in oil and when it heat up put chicken wing inside to fried it to golden.

7)When it done pour in a bottle of coca cola,cooking wine,soy sauce,oyster sauce, prepared ginger, scallion and garlic slice.

8)Boil it for 20min in medium fire.

9)When 20min is up turn to high fire for 1 min and you done!
